About
Mission

Our mission is to produce highly predictable, reliable, and robust real-time software for diverse markets. We strive to offer innovative services, products, and solutions that help our clients achieve business success in shorter timeframes.

Objectives

Our roadmap for 2010-2012 focuses on constant improvement and adaptation to customer needs. We also plan to release new products, including a Graphical User Interface and FAT File System support.

Company Profile

Founded in Poland in 2009, SpaceShadow develops software for embedded and real-time applications. We create universal solutions that can be successfully applied across various industries.

Our flagship product, Sirius RTOS, ensures that customers can execute real-time tasks with maximum efficiency and reliability, significantly shortening development time for embedded designs.
